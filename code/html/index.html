The gym

In our three-story building, you will discover a comprehensive selection of top-quality exercise equipment tailored to your preferences. Regardless of your workout regimen, we offer a diverse array of machines to accommodate your needs. Furthermore, we have allocated designated areas within our facility for post-workout stretching sessions, allowing for optimal recovery and relaxation following strenuous exercise routines.

Group workout

We host multiple sessions for you to attend at your own convenience, providing an ideal setting for group workouts designed to enhance your fitness journey. Our facilities offer a wide range of equipment tailored specifically for collaborative exercise sessions. Whether you prefer cardio, strength training, or specialized routines, we provide ample resources to accommodate diverse fitness interests.

Personal training

We offer personalized training sessions designed to meet your individual fitness goals. Our experienced trainers provide tailored workouts and guidance to help you achieve optimal results. Whether you're aiming for weight loss, muscle gain, or improved overall fitness, our one-on-one sessions are customized to suit your specific needs. With flexible scheduling options, you can book sessions at your convenience and receive dedicated attention from our certified trainers. Invest in your health and fitness journey with our personalized training programs.

Sauna

Indulge in the rejuvenating experience of our gym's sauna facilities. Step into a serene environment where relaxation and wellness converge. Our sauna offers a tranquil retreat from the rigors of your workout routine, providing a space to unwind and rejuvenate both body and mind. Whether you seek relief from muscle tension, stress reduction, or simply a moment of tranquility, our sauna is the perfect sanctuary. Enjoy the therapeutic benefits of heat therapy in a comfortable and inviting atmosphere. Enhance your gym experience with the restorative power of our sauna facilities.
