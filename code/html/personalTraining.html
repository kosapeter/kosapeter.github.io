Presonal training

We offer personalized training sessions designed to meet your individual fitness goals. Our experienced trainers provide tailored workouts and guidance to help you achieve optimal results. Whether you're aiming for weight loss, muscle gain, or improved overall fitness, our one-on-one sessions are customized to suit your specific needs. With flexible scheduling options, you can book sessions at your convenience and receive dedicated attention from our certified trainers. Invest in your health and fitness journey with our personalized training programs.

To reserve a session with a trainer, individuals must contact the trainer via email or chat for approval prior to booking. Each session, contingent upon the trainer's availability, is tailored to individual needs and incurs an additional $10 fee. Reservations can be made up to one week in advance, and cancellations must be made at least one day prior to the scheduled session. Failure to cancel without attendance will incur a $5 fee. To secure a session, ensure you have a valid ticket or purchase a day pass if needed, and remember to be logged in.
