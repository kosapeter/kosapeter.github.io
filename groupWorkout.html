Group workout

We host multiple sessions for you to attend at your own convenience, providing an ideal setting for group workouts designed to enhance your fitness journey. Our facilities offer a wide range of equipment tailored specifically for collaborative exercise sessions. Whether you prefer cardio, strength training, or specialized routines, we provide ample resources to accommodate diverse fitness interests.

To join a group workout session, reservations are mandatory, with each session limited to 20 participants. Reservations can be made up to one week prior to the session, and cancellations are accepted up to one day in advance. Failure to cancel a reservation without attendance will result in a $5 fine. To secure a reservation, ensure you are logged in, possess a valid ticket that does not expire before the session, or alternatively, purchase a day pass.
